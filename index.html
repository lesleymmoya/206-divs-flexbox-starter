Hi, I'm Lesley

I'm a Responsive Designer and Developer based in Taos, New Mexico who thrives on creating unique visuals. When I am not designing, I can be found learning new tech and design skills, walking my dogs, trying out new recipes or hanging out with friends.

My passions: I love animals. I currently have 2 dogs and 4 cats. I love modern homes and interior design. I stalk ebay and Craigslist for Mid Century Modern pieces.
